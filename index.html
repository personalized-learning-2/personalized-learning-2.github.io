What is PLUS?

PLUS is software designed to improve learning outcomes for K-12 students by combining the power and benefits of both human and computer tutoring. The PL² system uses research-driven mentor training along with artificial intelligence software designed to improve mentor efficiency. By syncing with students' existing math learning software, PL² provides mentors with personalized resources with a click of a button.
