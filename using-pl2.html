A Story Of How PLUS; Works
Adela is a mentor that works with students who need extra help in math class. She meets with them once a week, one-on-one or in small groups. Adela's students use an online program that they use to practice math problems and learn concepts, both in school and at home. Adela works with each student to set personal goals for the number of activities they'll complete and the progress they'll make. PLUS; connects with the online program to help Adela track her students' learning.
PLUS; Features
Dashboard
PLUS; allows mentors to view all their students and set individual goals based on their status.
Resources
PLUS; recommends resources to the mentors based on the status of their students.
Tracking Progress
Using PLUS;, mentors can track how their students' ed-tech data changes over time.
