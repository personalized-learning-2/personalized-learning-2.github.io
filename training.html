The PL2 Training program is based on in-house research by scientists at Carnegie Mellon University. Our evidence-based program covers 13 different competencies, across 5 strategic areas, that our research shows are needed for an educator to be succesful.

The 5 areas are:
  • Social Emotional Learning
  • Mastering Content
  • Advocacy
  • Relationships
  • Technology Tools
