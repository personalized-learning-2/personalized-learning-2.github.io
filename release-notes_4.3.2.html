PLUS 4.3.2 Release Notes

10/12/2022

More new lessons have been added to PLUS training! We've also incorporated feedback from our partners in this release - specifically, in providing better feedback to you!

4.3.2 Update List

  PLUS Training - New Lesson Content

We've designed and released four new modules for PLUS Lessons:

  • Managing Time Effectively
  • Exploring Implicit Bias
  • Avoiding Unconscious Assumptions
  • Adjusting Task Difficulty
  Lesson Feedback

  • When reviewing completed Lessons, multiple choice questions will now display correctness feedback, with incorrect responses highlighted red and correct responses highlighted green.
