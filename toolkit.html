Personalized Tools to Support
Students

For a truly personalized learning experience, student's effort and progress must be measured in tandem with their personal, motivational, social and emotional conditions.

The PL² web application combines multi-modal data about students from different sources such as the math software they use for practice, self evaluations, tutor reflections, and more. The PL² system then recommends personalized interventions to tutors and educators for each student to guide them towards success.
